# 5S Ocean Drifter

## 🌊 Overview
The ocean has been one of humanity's greatest mysteries since before recorded history. From the Portuguese explorers braving the raging seas to modern oil tankers demanding ever-evolving technology for safer and smoother sailing, the ocean has played a crucial role in shaping our world.

Despite our advancements, scientists believe that only **5% of the ocean has been explored** with current technology. This means that humanity knows as much about our vast sky as we do about our own seas. 

The **5S Ocean Drifter** is an innovative piece of equipment designed to acquire data from **surface sea currents**, expanding oceanographic knowledge and enhancing our understanding of marine dynamics.

## 🚀 Importance of Ocean Exploration
A deeper understanding of the ocean leads to significant advancements in various fields, including:
- **Safety** – Improved navigation and hazard detection.
- **Security** – Enhanced monitoring of maritime activity.
- **Efficiency** – Better route planning for ships and energy-saving innovations.

## 🔬 What is 5S?
**5S (Sensoring System for Surface Sea Streams)** is a **low-cost, low-power** solution designed to autonomously collect ocean data for extended periods. The drifter gathers crucial data, including:

- **GPS Coordinates** – To track current direction and average velocity.
- **Water Temperature** – To study thermal variations.
- **Accelerometer Data** – To measure wave intensity and movement patterns.

## 📡 Data Transmission & Storage
The collected data will be transmitted via a **yet-to-be-defined communication protocol** in **JSON format**, ensuring seamless integration with an existing **database** for real-time analysis and research.

## 🌍 Future Impact
By deploying the 5S Ocean Drifter, we can contribute to a better understanding of ocean currents, climate change, and maritime operations, pushing the boundaries of oceanic exploration.
